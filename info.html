Triage SOP

Emergency Department Triage Pathway

Standard protocols for zoning patients entering the Emergency Department based on severity:

  • 🟢 Green (Non-Critical): Minor ailments, stable vitals. Max wait target: 4h.
  • 🟡 Yellow (Semi-Critical): Moderate distress, potential threat. Max wait target: 4h (Seen) / 6h (Admitted).
  • 🔴 Red (Critical/Resus): Threatened airway, hemodynamically unstable. Immediate response.
Referral

FMC/PKU Referral Checklist

Ensure these details are ready before filling in the Referral Form:

  • Ensure Patient Name & MRN are double-checked.
  • Identify the referring Doctor (FMC/PKU name) and the receiving MO.
  • Select the correct target Primary Team (e.g. Med, Surgical, Ortho, O&G).
  • Define the clinical Plan and target zone (YZ/RZ/Non).
Handover

SBAR Handover Protocol

Use the structured SBAR template during handovers to departments:

  • S (Situation): Patient name, age, MRN, diagnosis.
  • B (Background): Brief history, allergies, relevant investigations.
  • A (Assessment): Current vitals, GCS, treatments given.
  • R (Recommendation): Explicit plan, pending labs, target team.
